About Me

hello 👋

I am a Software Development Engineer at Credit-Suisse. I have completed my bachelors in Computer Science from University of Pune (now Savitribai Phule Pune University) 🎓 with deep interest in Python, Go, Backend Development and DevOps. I have always enjoyed to solve problems and building something since I was young. That's how I look at this field of Software Development with vast amount of problems and how these problems can be solved keeping in mind about the impact it will have on humanity.

My Journey 👨‍💻

I learned how to code from different online and offline courses, Youtube, codeacademy and trial & error method. Each project that I build gave me unique experience and taught me new skills. I started my journey by learning Android Development, followed by Data Science and DevOps. I haven't stop learning and with the evolution of new tech, I am very excited about the new innovations happening all around the world.

For Opportunities 🤝

I am looking for amazing opportunities in the fields of Backend Development and/or DevOps. Do you want to work together? Please reach out to me by e-mail or connect with me on LinkedIn
